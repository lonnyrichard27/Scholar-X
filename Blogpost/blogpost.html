Opportuniy Hub with Bola lawal:Don't let lack of Funds limit your dreams of higher education

We need more young Africans to pursue career paths in these fields. And to achieve this, they will have to pursue university degress in reputable schools either at home or abroad. However, there's a major issue as regards the cost across the globe.


While this is a global phenomenon that students and their parents must deal with, a simple answer is: SCHOLARSHIPS! Scholarships offer needing students the opportunity of realising their dreams of attaining a quality education. Whether they wish to study in their home (Africa) or abroad, there are thousands of scholarships they can apply for.


The great Nelson Mandela once said; "Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world" and this has never been truer for Africa. We live in a time where we need to build for the future of Africa - a future where we create solutions to solve issues like poverty, illiteracy, poor sanitation, malnutrition and so on.


We need a well educated youth population - particularly in regards to Science Technology Enginerring and Mathematics (STEM). We need more young Africans to pursue career paths in these fields.
